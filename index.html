Biography

Takanori Hara received the B.Eng. degree from National Institution for Academic Degrees and Quality Enhancement of Higher Education in 2016 and the M.Eng. degree from Nara Institute of Science and Technology, Japan, in 2018. He is currently working towards the Ph.D degree with Nara Institute of Science and Technology, Japan. He is also a Research Fellow of the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (DC2). His research interests include evacuation guiding, route planning, and game-theoretic approach.
